My UX Skills

Prototyping

Finding the balance between functionality and aesthetic is one of the most satisfying parts of UX/UI. When you can find that balance it is the most satisfying feeling in the world. But, is also one of the most frustrating when something you worked on doesnt turn out the way you want.

User Testing

No matter how well you think something is working having someone who has never touched this product can really tell what is going right and what is going wrong. While also finding underlying problems that werent even considered in initial testing

Heuristics

Creating a happy path is the ultimate goal for a designer. It is what makes or breaks a site. This is what makes websites truly stand out. They look nothing alike but when you take a closer look you find, identical layouts that allow for every type of user to use. This creates a challenge for a designer and is something I really enjoy solving
